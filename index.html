Understanding the Categories

Participates in Group Activities

Alumni are actively involved in group activities, such as discussions, workshops, or networking events.

Lack of Engaging Content

Alumni found the content shared on the groups uninteresting or not relevant to their interests or needs.

Engaged with Other Activities

Alumni were interested but had scheduling conflicts or were committed to other personal or professional activities.

No Data

Alumni could'nt afford to buy data to access the internet and participate in the activities.

Device Issues

Technical problems prevented participation, such as connectivity issues, incompatible devices, or software problems.

No longer Interested in WAVE

Alumni have indicated they are no longer interested in participating in the WAVE program or related initiatives.

Others

Reasons that don't fit into the other categories or unique circumstances affecting alumni engagement.

No Response

Alumni did not respond to the survey, or follow-up communications.
